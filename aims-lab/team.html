Y-AIMS Laboratory

Our Team

A multidisciplinary group of researchers working at the intersection of materials science, physics, chemistry, biology, molecular modeling, and artificial intelligence.

Principal Investigator

YY

Prof. Yaroslava (Yara) G. Yingling

Kobe Steel Distinguished Professor of MSE University Faculty Fellow Associate Department Head Director of Undergraduate Program Director, Graduate Certificate in Materials Informatics

Prof. Yingling received a B.S. in Computer Science and Engineering from St. Petersburg State Technical University (1996) and a Ph.D. in Materials Engineering and High-Performance Computing from Penn State (2002). She completed postdoctoral research at Penn State Chemistry and the NIH National Cancer Institute before joining NC State University in 2007. Her research integrates molecular modeling, data science, and AI to accelerate materials discovery for energy, environment, and health applications.

yara_yingling@ncsu.edu +1 (919) 513-2624 3014 Engineering Building I, NC State
