About Y-AIMS

Where Computation Meets Discovery

Welcome to the Y-AIMS Laboratory at North Carolina State University. We develop and deploy AI workflows and multiscale molecular modeling methods for predictive and data-efficient materials understanding and design.

Our research spans density functional theory (DFT), all-atom molecular dynamics (MD), and coarse-grained approaches — bridging atomistic insight with machine learning to unlock new materials faster.

Biomimetic Materials

Prof. Yaroslava Yingling

Associate Department Head · University Faculty Scholar · NC State University

Prof. Yingling leads the Y-AIMS Laboratory, developing AI workflows and multiscale molecular modeling methods for materials discovery. Her research spans biomimetic materials, nanomaterials, and data-driven materials design with over 100 publications and 130+ invited talks worldwide.
